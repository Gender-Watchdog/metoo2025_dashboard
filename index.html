This dashboard documents not only Dongguk University's sexual violence crisis, institutional cover-ups, and alleged misuse of public funds, but also exposes how these issues are systemic across Korean higher education. Title IX compliance risks are now an existential threat to Korean universities as U.S. partners re-evaluate relationships, ATIXA has been notified about multiple institutions, and the KWDI report shows 61.5% of female arts students are victims of sexual violence (65.5% by faculty, who are predominantly male).
